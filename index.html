About Me

I am an American-Canadian living in Galloway, Scotland with Matt Kusner and our two senior rescue dogs, Nora Ruth and Karl T . I was a public health scientist, and am now a writer and outdoor guide.

Guiding

I learned to love the outdoors later in life- I did not grow up in a place or culture that gave me opportunities to learn about hiking, camping, or the joy of sitting under an ancient tree.

Because of this, I am interested in working with folks who want to be in the outdoors, but feel worried about their skills, or worry they might not belong. I welcome ANYONE who wants to walk- I am LGBT+ friendly, and welcome those from diverse backgrounds.

I am a Galloway and Southern Ayrshire UNESCO Biosphere trained guide, am a Leave No Trace practitioner, and have my outdoor first aid qualification.

I specialise in chill walking, and offer two walks in the Galloway Forest Park. Learn more at Galloway Slow Walks.
